Quality Education

This goal seeks to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. It emphasizes the importance of free primary and secondary education, access to quality early childhood development, and vocational training. The goal targets the elimination of gender disparities in education and aims to provide equal access to education for marginalized groups, including persons with disabilities, indigenous peoples, and children in vulnerable situations. It also focuses on improving the quality of education by enhancing teacher training, learning facilities, and educational resources, and promoting global citizenship and sustainable development.
