Clean water & Sanitation

This goal ensures availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. It emphasizes the need for universal access to safe and affordable drinking water, adequate sanitation, and hygiene. The goal focuses on improving water quality by reducing pollution, minimizing waste, and protecting water-related ecosystems. It also addresses the efficient use of water resources, promoting water-use efficiency across all sectors. Efforts include expanding international cooperation and capacity-building to support developing countries in water and sanitation-related activities and programs, including water harvesting, desalination, water efficiency, and wastewater treatment.
