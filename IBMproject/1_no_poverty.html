NO POVERTY

This goal aims to eradicate poverty in all its forms and dimensions by 2030. It recognizes that poverty goes beyond lack of income and resources, encompassing deprivation in well-being and access to basic services like education, healthcare, and clean water. The goal emphasizes the need for social protection systems, sustainable economic growth, and employment opportunities for all, especially the most vulnerable. It also focuses on building resilience among those who are impoverished and mitigating their exposure to climate-related events and economic, social, and environmental shocks.
