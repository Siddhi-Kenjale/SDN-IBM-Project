PARTNERSHIP FOR GOALS

This goal aims to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development. It emphasizes the need for strong global partnerships and cooperation to achieve the SDGs. The goal focuses on mobilizing financial resources, promoting technology transfer, and enhancing capacity-building in developing countries. It also addresses the importance of promoting a fair, open, and rules-based trading system, improving global macroeconomic stability, and enhancing policy coherence for sustainable development.
