Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

This goal aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation. It emphasizes the importance of quality, reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure, including regional and transborder infrastructure, to support economic development and human well-being. The goal focuses on promoting sustainable industrialization and significantly increasing the industry's share of employment and GDP. It also addresses the need for enhanced scientific research, technological innovation, and the development of new industries. Efforts include promoting access to information and communication technology and expanding infrastructure and industrial development in developing countries.
