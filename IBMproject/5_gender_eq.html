Gender Equality

This goal aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. It seeks to end all forms of discrimination and violence against women and girls, including trafficking and sexual exploitation. The goal emphasizes the need for equal access to education, healthcare, decent work, and representation in political and economic decision-making processes. It also focuses on eliminating harmful practices like child marriage and female genital mutilation. Efforts include promoting women's rights and gender equality laws, policies, and programs, and ensuring women's full participation and equal opportunities in leadership and decision-making roles.
