Decent work & Economic growth

This goal promotes sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. It emphasizes the need for economic diversification, technological innovation, and the promotion of entrepreneurship. The goal focuses on improving global resource efficiency in consumption and production, and decoupling economic growth from environmental degradation. It also targets the elimination of forced labor, human trafficking, and child labor, and the promotion of safe and secure working environments. Efforts include ensuring equal pay for work of equal value, promoting policies that support job creation, and strengthening social protection systems.
