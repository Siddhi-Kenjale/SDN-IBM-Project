Affordable & Clean energy

This goal aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. It promotes the expansion of renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, and hydropower, and emphasizes the need for energy efficiency and the development of sustainable energy infrastructure. The goal focuses on increasing the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix, improving access to modern energy services, and promoting clean energy technologies. It also addresses the need for international cooperation and investment in energy infrastructure, particularly in developing countries, to ensure a sustainable and inclusive energy transition.
