SUSTAINABLE CITIES & COMUNITIES

This goal aims to make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. It emphasizes the need for adequate, safe, and affordable housing and basic services, as well as upgrading slums. The goal focuses on sustainable urban planning and management, improving urban infrastructure, and reducing the environmental impact of cities, including air quality and waste management. It also addresses the importance of protecting cultural and natural heritage, ensuring access to safe and inclusive green and public spaces, and promoting disaster risk reduction. Efforts include fostering social inclusion and enhancing the capacity of local governments for sustainable urban development.
